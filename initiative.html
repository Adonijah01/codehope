Our Purpose

Mission

To bridge the digital divide in rural Kenya through inclusive and accessible tech education, empowering the youth of West Pokot with skills that transform their futures and communities.

Vision

A generation of digitally empowered youth rising from West Pokot, creating solutions for local challenges and competing in the global digital economy.

Our Story

May 2025 – Humble Beginnings

Inspired by the growing digital divide in West Pokot, the idea of CodeHope Pokot was born. A small team began dreaming of a community-based tech initiative focused on equipping disadvantaged children and girls with essential digital skills.

June 2025 – Call for Support

Outreach officially began for sponsors, donors, and volunteers to bring the vision to life. We are seeking laptops, funding, internet access, and teaching support to launch our very first community class.
“With just one laptop, we could spark a future. Imagine what ten can do.”

Future Goals – Expanding Reach

Once launched, our goal is to establish community-based learning centers and mobile tech labs to serve even the most remote areas of West Pokot.

Future Vision – Girls Who Code Pokot

We're planning a dedicated program to encourage young girls to embrace coding, STEM, and leadership through hands-on learning, mentorship, and storytelling from female tech leaders.

Next Milestone – First Launch

With your support, we aim to launch our first training center by late 2025. Every donation, laptop, and helping hand brings us closer to that goal.
